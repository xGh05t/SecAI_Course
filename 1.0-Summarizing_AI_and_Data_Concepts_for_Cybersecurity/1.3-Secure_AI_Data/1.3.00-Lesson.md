
# 1.3 Secure AI Data

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Exam Objectives:** 
> 1.2 Explain the importance of data security in relation to AI.

AI in cybersecurity relies heavily on keeping data secure at every stage, from collection to storage and sharing. It's important to use solid data handling techniques (like encryption, access controls, and regular audits) to prevent leaks or unauthorized access. Understanding key AI data concepts, such as labeling, anonymizing, and responsible training, plays a pivotal role in safeguarding information. Strong data security doesn't just protect sensitive details; it also ensures that your AI models operate accurately and can be trusted to make reliable decisions.
