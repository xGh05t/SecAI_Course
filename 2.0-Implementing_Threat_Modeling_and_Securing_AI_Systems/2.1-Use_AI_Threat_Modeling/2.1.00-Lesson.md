
# 2.1 Use AI Threat Modeling

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Exam Objectives**
> - 2.1 Given a scenario, use AI threat-modeling resources.

AI threat modeling is becoming a must-have skill for cybersecurity professionals, enabling one to pinpoint and address risks specific to AI systems. It's smart to tap into available AI threat resources—which can include guides, tools, and databases—to stay ahead of emerging challenges. By applying established threat modeling frameworks, you can build a structured approach to uncovering vulnerabilities. Typically, the process involves identifying assets, spotting potential threats, and then figuring out how to mitigate those risks to keep your AI systems secure and resilient.
